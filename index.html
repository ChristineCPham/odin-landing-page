This website is dogsome

This website is all about my dog, Tinkerbear. She is a four year old border collie. Tinkerbear is a good girl and she can prove it!

Who is Tinkerbear?

Tinkerbear enjoys playing every second she can. As long as there is a toy nearby, she will not rest. Tinkerbear likes balls and squeaky toys the most.

As an intellectual, Tinkerbear places an high important on her education. Her favorite classes are: Human Training 101, Stealing Food 301, and Chasing Cats 201.

Tinkerbear has mastered the art of begging. With in five minutes, she can convince guests to give her a day's worth of food. Her owner does not approve of this behavior.

Tinkerbear is a big scaredy-cat. She will take refuge under the bed whenever she hears fireworks. If she hears them outdoors, she will run behind large men.
